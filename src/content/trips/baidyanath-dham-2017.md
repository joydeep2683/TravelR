# Baidyanath Dham Yatra

Our journey began from Kolkata, West Bengal aka the city of joy. Our train journey was smooth and comfortable. We reached Deoghar, the spiritual center of Jharkhand at night.

## The Baidyanath Dham
The Baidyanath Dham is a pilgrimage site located in Deoghar, Jharkhand, India. It is one of the most important pilgrimage sites in India and is considered to be one of the most sacred places in the country. It is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas in India and is said to be the birthplace of Lord Shiva. Also know as Baba Dham.

Board the train to Kolkata. By Shatabdi Express.

A truly rejuvenating experience for everyone involved.
